Journey's Beginning

This is my journey through Om’s River. It’s a meditation, a place to explore thoughts, questions and maybe a discover a few answers. In the world we live today, there’s plenty to distract us. Then finding answers is not so clear. Meditation can be a way to find those answers. But it is not the drama that we’ve made it out to be - it is a simple process to sit quietly and undistracted by the world around you, then let thoughts come, but most importantly to let them go as well.
